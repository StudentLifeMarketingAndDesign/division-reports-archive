Inflatables Offer Exciting Recreation Opportunity for UI Students

The Campus Recreation and Wellness Center (CRWC) played host to an inflatable obstacle course course throughout the year, giving University of Iowa students an exciting way to spend time in the state of the art natatorium. The inflatable obstacle course features a 24-foot-tall inflatable slide located in the diving well.
